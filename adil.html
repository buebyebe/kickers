About the Reebok brand

In 1958, two of the founder's grandchildren, Joe and Jeff Foster, founded the Reebok companion company in Bolton. In 1979, at the Chicago International Sneaker Show, American businessman Paul Fayerman drew attention to Reebok. He signed a deal to license and distribute the Reebok brand in the United States.
